Bibliography

My name is Simon Rahnasto and I love programming. My first encounter with it was back in 2010, when I started learning Blizzards 'GalaxyScript' in order to create custom maps for the popular RTS title 'SC2'. With it I created a custom game named 'The Final Stand', which at it's peak reached the second most played custom map in the European Region (and 7th in the US). Ever since then I have been hooked to programming and I really enjoy learning new languages, paradigms, patterns, algorithms and datastructures. I am currently a student at Stockholm University in my final Semester. In my free time I enjoy creating Programming tutorials, working on personal projects and playing RTS games.
